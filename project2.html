About Coding Master

Think about the job you have right now and the details of how it gets done. Were those details or even your job around 5 years ago, 10 years ago? There was not a degree offered in college to prepare Seth, Joe or Dustin for their first companies they would own. The need was created through emerging technology and a changing consumer market. But at the foundation of those companies were sound computer algorithms, unchanging technology logic and a lot of programming. Technology seems to release a new ‘boom’ every six months. It can be difficult to understand how to get involved and start contributing. Always keep in mind that the foundation of good business principles and (in technology) sound computer logic with remain the same. Simply Coding’s mission is to prepare youth with the core principles of how all software is created so they can feel confident and be able to contribute to technology of the future.
